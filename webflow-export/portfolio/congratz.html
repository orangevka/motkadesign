Congratz

The app and landing page for the gift card service.

Congratz is a new app that revolutionizes digital gifting.
You can send virtual VISA/Mastercard cards with personalized designs, valued up to $500. Our design objective was to establish a distinctive brand identity and develop a user-friendly mobile app interface.  The app interface, crafted by Motka, successfully underwent rigorous UX testing and has been published on the App Store.

Logo

Symbolizing good news, our logo features a charming bird saying:
You’ve got a gift!
The inclusion of a crown subtly suggests that the gift may hold significant value ;-)

Illustrations

The project team from the very beginning focused on design as the cornerstone of the entire project. The MVP of the application and the landing page were developed within 5 weeks. As a result, Congratz obtained a recognizable branding and a user-friendly interface with a high conversion rate to purchases.
