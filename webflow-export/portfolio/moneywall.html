Moneywall

We have developed a brand identity design, a new landing page, and a payment service UI for a platform that facilitates enables breaking payments into smaller installments.

Payment schedule

The payment schedule is the key feature of the service, consistently displayed throughout the entire process. It provides a clear overview of the three payment parts, the current payment stage, and the amount already paid.

Background and Graphics

The payment schedule chart is a key element in the Moneywall branding. It is utilized used as a background and can be customized with any color from the brand’s palette. To create a dynamic and visually striking composition, one part of the chart is emphasized.

This approach gives the Moneywall brand a cohesive and unified appearance in all digital communications. We designed a landing page based on this style and created user interface templates for both desktop and mobile.

Graphics

The logo and minimalist graphics seamlessly adapt to various platforms while maintaining their recognizability. The concept revolves around the idea of traveling now and paying later.

Payment Scenario

The installment payment process is simple and convenient, allowing for seamless integration into any car rental or airline ticket purchasing service.

Bright, Easy, Lively

Six vibrant and complementary colors set the desired mood and can be used in marketing materials in various combinations. Each color can function as both a background and an accent. To create a distinctive image, it is recommended to use at least two colors. We have prepared a brand guidelines book that provides detailed instructions on utilizing the brand graphics effectively.
